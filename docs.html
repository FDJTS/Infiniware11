introduction

infiniware is a human-centric software ecosystem. we prioritize focus, privacy, and technical excellence over growth and engagement. our tools are designed to be silent companions in your digital workflow.

privacy & sovereignty

privacy is our first and most important rule. **we never sell data.** we believe in users owning their own digital footprint. every project under the infiniware banner is built with a "privacy first" mindset, ensuring that zero unnecessary data leaves your device.

design rules

all infiniware interfaces follow a strict structural hierarchy:

  • all text lowercase: except for the "I" in the logo.
  • typography: ubuntu for branding, inter for utility.
  • colors: monochromatic palette with red as a structural accent.

authentication system

we use firebase authentication for identity management. users can authenticate via email or github oauth. account deletion is mandatory and structural, purging all associated data.
