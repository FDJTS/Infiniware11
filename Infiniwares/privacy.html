03 // privacy

your data is yours.

core privacy philosophy

at **infiniware**, we believe privacy isn't a feature—it's a fundamental right. our data collection strategy is simple: we don't want what we don't need. your behavioral data is not an asset to us; your trust is.

1. what we collect

- **authentication data**: for email signups, we store your email address and a securely hashed password. for github auth, we store your username and profile picture url for personalization. - **content**: any posts or comments you intentionally share in the community tab are stored in our secure database.

2. what we do NOT collect

- we do not use third-party tracking pixels (e.g., meta, google ads). - we do not sell or lease user data under any circumstances. - we do not track your location externally (al-muslim location data stays local on your device).

3. technical security

we use industry-standard encryption for data transit (tls) and at rest. security is built into the architecture from the first line of code, prioritizing isolation and minimal access privileges.

account deletion

you have full control. you can request a complete data purge or account deletion at any time through your dashboard or by contacting support.
